About Us

Mwambani Hospital was founded in 1969 by the Francican Sisters of Denekamp of Netherland; whose top most founder was the late Sister Tarcisius. It is a Roman Catholic Institution in the Diocese of Mbeya. In 1972 it became District Disignated Hospital for Chunya District Council. Mwambani Hospital is situated at Mwambani village, Mkwajuni Ward, Songwe Division in the newly established Songwe District Council. In the 1980 the Franscan Sister of Mahenge came to Mwambani with the objective to take over from the Franciscan Sister of Denekamp. The Diocese Sisters our lady queen of apposties of Mbeya (Shirika la Bikira Malkia wa Mitume) were the third congregation involved in providing the Health Service in the Hospital. The hospital is under Roman Catholic Diocese of Mbeya.
